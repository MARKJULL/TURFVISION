Product · TurfVision

The first eye on the turf.

A forward-facing infrared sensor that mounts on any hydraulic-equipped turf machine. It watches the surface ahead — continuously, in every light condition — and triggers an on-machine alarm the moment hydraulic oil meets the grass.

Configuration

Alert. Or Alert plus Shutdown.

Every TurfVision unit is an alert system. The optional Shutdown tier adds automatic hydraulic power cut on detection — for fleets that operate on the highest-value surfaces.

Technical

Specifications.

Built for the heat, vibration, and weather that turf equipment lives in. Every detail chosen for reliability in the field — not lab conditions.

01 / SENSOR

Long-wave infrared

Forward-facing LWIR sensor reads turf surface temperature. Optimized for the thermal differential between hydraulic oil and cool turf canopy.

02 / DETECTION

Patent-pending algorithm

Anomaly detection tuned to the bloom signature of hot hydraulic fluid on grass. Rejects false positives from sun-warmed cart paths and metal surfaces.

03 / RESPONSE

On-machine alarm

Audible and high-visibility light. Zero dependency on operator phones, apps, or network connectivity. The alert reaches the cab in milliseconds.

04 / POWER

Native machine power

Draws from the machine’s 12V or 24V system. No batteries to replace. No charging cycle. Runs whenever the machine runs.

05 / ENVIRONMENT

IP-rated enclosure

Sealed against water, dust, and the abuse of irrigation, debris, and daily wash-down. Vibration-tolerant mounting for full-fairway operation.

06 / INSTALL

Dealer installed

Mounting and integration handled by certified Toro, John Deere, or Jacobsen dealer technicians. Typical install: under 90 minutes per machine.
