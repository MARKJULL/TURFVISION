How TurfVision Works

Four steps. No app required.

TurfVision is a forward-facing infrared sensor that mounts on any hydraulic-equipped turf machine. It runs whenever the machine runs.

01 / SCAN

IR sensor scans ahead

Forward-facing infrared reads turf surface temperature continuously while the machine operates. All light conditions — dawn, dusk, full sun, cloudy.

02 / DETECT

Thermal anomaly identified

The heat signature of hydraulic oil on cool turf is unmistakable. The detection algorithm reads the bloom and confirms within milliseconds.

03 / ALERT

On-machine alarm fires

Audible and visual alarm activates on the cab immediately. No phone. No app. No connectivity. The operator knows before the next pass.

04 / RESPOND

Operator acts. Damage stops.

Machine is stopped or routed to a cart path. With the optional Shutdown tier, hydraulic power cuts automatically. The stripe never appears.
